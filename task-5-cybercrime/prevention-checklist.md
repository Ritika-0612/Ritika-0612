# Stay Safe Online – Checklist


•	Use strong passwords that are hard to guess, and avoid using the same password everywhere. 

•	Turn on two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible for extra security. 

•	Never share your OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone, even if they sound official. 

•	Be careful before clicking on links or downloading files, especially from unknown sources. 

•	Always double-check who is contacting you before replying or sharing any information. 

•	Keep your phone and laptop updated so they stay protected from new threats. 

  **UPI / Payment Safety**

•	Don’t accept unknown payment requests on UPI apps—receiving money doesn’t need approval. 

•	Avoid scanning random QR codes or entering details on suspicious payment pages. 

  **General Awareness**

•	Think before posting personal details on social media. 

•	Use only trusted apps and official websites for payments and important work. 

  **Where to Report**

If something goes wrong, act quickly:

•	National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in 

•	Helpline Number: 1930
