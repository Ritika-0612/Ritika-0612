# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

**Do’s**

-Think twice before you post anything online

-Keep your profile simple, respectful, and a bit professional

-Use privacy settings to control who can see your posts

-Share positive, useful, or meaningful content

-Be polite and respectful while interacting with others


**Don’ts**

-Don’t share personal details like phone number or location publicly

-Don’t post anything offensive or inappropriate

-Don’t believe or forward information without checking it

-Don’t get into unnecessary arguments online

-Don’t accept friend requests from unknown peopl
